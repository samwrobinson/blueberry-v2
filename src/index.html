This season, Blueberry Lake Cross Country Skiing is undergoing an important period of transition. As such, we will exclusively offer season passes & day passes for our visitors.

The lodge will remain open for visitor use, including restroom access, but will not be regularly staffed. This means that equipment rentals will not be available on a regular basis. While not regularly staffed, you can easily purchase tickets through our virtual payment system (via QR codes posted on-site) or online before your visit. Full service of the lodge, including staffing and equipment rentals will be available during holiday periods and by appointment.

We’re excited to maintain access to our trails, which will remain open 7 days a week for skiing, snowshoeing, and more.

We appreciate your understanding and continued support as we navigate these changes and work toward an even brighter future for our community of outdoor enthusiasts.

Skiing at Blueberry lake

Located in the Mad River Valley, Vermont

Discover the warmth and charm of our sheltered trails! With 35 kilometers across 11 trails, you can enjoy classic skiing, skating, snowshoeing, and even fat biking. From beginner-friendly paths to expert-level routes, our trails offer something for everyone. Wander through serene wooded areas or glide through wide-open meadows with breathtaking views.

Bring the whole family along! And for those with four-legged friends, well-behaved dogs are welcome—just be sure to clean up after them!

Come and explore, play, and make lasting memories on our trails.
