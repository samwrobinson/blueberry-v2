Come check it out! East side trails were all groomed and tracked last night and have set up beautifully. Things will soften up through the day in these temps so earlier will definitely be better than later. The groomers are currently out on the west side so everything should be groomed and tracked shortly. Right now it's basically perfect spring skiing; corn snow on a very solid base. There are a few spots that are thinking about icing up. I would suggest skiing 6 and 8 in a counter clockwise direct and there is a little patch of ice right at the end of the bridge heading out to 9. Other than that I think it should be pretty great. Come and get it while it lasts!
