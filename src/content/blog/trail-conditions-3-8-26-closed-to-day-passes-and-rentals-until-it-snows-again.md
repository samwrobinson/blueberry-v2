The big melt out has arrived. There is still a ton of snow out there and if you are a pass holder and you want to go ski you are very welcome to, but if I was you I would pick a different activity for the day. We did try to get the machines around at least 1 and 2 this morning but trails are washing out in multiple places and we headed for home instead of heading out on 2. There may be fewer wash outs on the upper trails but it is too soft for us to get the drag around. We will be CLOSED to day passes and rentals today and every day until it snows again. Rumor has it there is some snow coming on Friday and Saturday so this might not be the end yet.
