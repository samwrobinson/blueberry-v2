Blueberry Lake Cross Country Ski Center

BBLXC is a small family run cross country ski center located in Vermont's Mad River Valley. We offer over 30 km of groomed/ tracked trails that can accommodate any skill level and full-service rentals of skis and snowshoes. Our trail network winds through both wooded areas and open meadows, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. The woodstove is always burning making the ski center a cozy place to warm up after your adventure and our staff is always working to make sure your experience at BBL is as positive and memorable as possible.

We welcome well-behaved dogs on our trails; please ensure they are under control and that you clean up after them.

For the latest trail conditions and updates, please refer to our TrailHub App, which provides real-time geolocation tracking on our GPS-mapped trails. We highly recommend downloading the app and loading the Blueberry Lake Cross Country trail system onto your mobile device for the most current information.

We appreciate your support of Blueberry Lake XC and look forward to providing you with a memorable outdoor experience at Blueberry Lake Cross Country Center!

New for the 2025/26 season

This year we are adding an 8 person sauna as well as an espresso bar. Don't worry! we aren't getting bougie! We just want to add a few things to make your time here even better. The vibes remain the same.

We have also invested in a new Tidd Teck G2 with track renovator in an effort to upgrade our grooming capabilities and will be working over the next few years to try to improve our trail network by leveling out the high spots and improving drainage.
