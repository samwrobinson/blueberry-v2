Sauna at Blueberry Lake XC

This year, we are excited to expand the nordic experience beyond skiing and snowshoeing! Our neighbords Jesse and Alicia are currently building an eight-person trailside sauna for us! The sauna will be open on weekends, holidays, and weekdays from 4 - 7 PM, or anytime by booking ahead for parties of two or more. Sauna appointments must be scheduled at least 1 hr in advance.

Sauna Season will run from December 1st - March 15th or until whenever the ski season closes, whichever comes later.

Monthly passes include unlimited sauna use for a 30-day period, and only existing customers who have purchased a season's ski passes are eligible for the discounted sauna add-on pricing.
